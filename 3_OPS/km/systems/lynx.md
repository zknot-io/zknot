# LYNX ALLYANCE — System Reference

## What it is
LYNX is a digital platform launched **January 2026** by the **Department of War (DoW)
Office of Small Business Programs (OSBP)**. Registration at **lynxconnect.io**.

Verified legitimate via official .gov sources (war.gov, business.defense.gov) and the
APEX Accelerator network on 2026-05-29.

## What it does (and doesn't)
- **Does:** structured company profile, readiness assessment / baseline, AI/ML-informed
  roadmap, partner + opportunity matching ("discovery").
- **Does NOT:** replace SAM.gov or any procurement portal. It is a **front door /
  on-ramp** that sits *before* the formal acquisition system. Registrations and
  solicitations still flow through standard procurement systems.

## Who it's for
New entrants, small businesses, non-traditional suppliers, and growing defense
contractors with **strong technical capability but limited defense-navigation
experience**. (i.e., ZKNOT's exact profile.)

## Key people (as of launch)
- Michael Cadenazzi — Assistant Secretary of War for Industrial Base Policy (ASW/IBP)
- James Mismash — Director, OSBP

## How to work it (ZKNOT playbook)
1. Verify legitimacy before entering identifiers — done.
2. Enter sensitive identifiers (UEI/CAGE/EIN) directly, never via any third party/chat.
3. Lead tag fields with custom tags; the canned taxonomy has no crypto/hardware terms.
4. Answer readiness honestly as an early-stage entrant — accurate baseline = useful roadmap.
5. Claim capabilities, not track record. No delivery-history claims pre-award.
6. Treat as a build-once asset, not a deadline.

## Federal-term glossary (encountered in the Advanced Business Info / FOCI section)

- **FOCI** — *Foreign Ownership, Control, or Influence.* Framework DoW uses to assess
  whether a foreign entity could access a contractor's work.
- **DCSA Facility Clearance** — *Defense Counterintelligence and Security Agency*
  clearance for a **facility** to handle classified info. Distinct from a personnel
  clearance. ("Hold" = action in process; not applicable if no clearance action open.)
- **SF-328** — *Certificate Pertaining to Foreign Interests.* The FOCI questionnaire
  tied to clearance/FOCI review.
- **DPAS** — *Defense Priorities and Allocations System.* Contract priority ratings
  (DO / DX) that let defense orders jump the production queue.
- **CFIUS** — *Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States.* Reviews foreign
  investment for national-security risk.
- **EO 12958** — Executive Order governing classified national security information
  (referenced in the classified-contract-history question).
- **FCI** — *Federal Contract Information.* Info provided by/generated for the government
  under a contract, not intended for public release.
- **CUI** — *Controlled Unclassified Information.* Sensitive but unclassified info
  requiring safeguarding. **Trigger for CMMC requirements.** Will appear in the
  Cybersecurity section — the section most likely to surface ZKNOT's real readiness gaps.

FAQ

Things people actually ask.

What if my dog/cat won't cooperate?

That is the most common situation, not the exception. Sessions are paced for the pet, not the clock — bring treats, toys, a favorite human, and we'll get there.

Do you photograph reactive or shy pets?

Yes. Tell me ahead of time what they're sensitive to (other dogs, strangers, certain sounds) and we'll plan a location and approach that works.

Where do shoots take place?

Salt Lake City and surrounding areas. I'll come to you, meet at a park, or somewhere meaningful — wherever your pet is most themselves.

How long until I get my photos?

Edited galleries typically arrive within [X] days. You'll get a preview link first to pick favorites.

What's the difference between "Session + Preview" and "Session + Photos"?

"Session + Preview" gets you the shoot and a preview gallery — but the digital files aren't released. It's the lower-commitment option. "Session + Photos" includes the full edited gallery delivered to you. You can always upgrade later.

How does the social media management work?

You send the photos, I handle the posting, captions, hashtags, and engagement. It's fully remote. Start with a single post or three, or set up a monthly subscription for ongoing growth.

Can you grow my pet's account from scratch?

Yes — but growth is a marathon. The Standard package or a monthly subscription is the right starting point if growth is the goal.

How do I pay?

Secure checkout via Stripe. Card details never touch this site directly.

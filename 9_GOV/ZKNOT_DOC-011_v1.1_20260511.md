### 3.1 Post-Certification Common Failure Points

Just because cert is in hand does not mean the work is done. New risks:

- **VA VIP registration delay**: Vets First eligibility specifically requires VIP, not just SAM. Federal contracting officers checking VIP find an empty profile until you register. The 5-day-post-validation window is real.
- **SAM-VetCert mismatch**: If names/EIN/address don't match exactly between VetCert record and SAM, federal CO searches may filter ZKNOT out. Spot-check after validation completes.
- **Stale CAP-001 distribution**: Sending a March 2026 CAP-001 to a CO post-cert wastes the introduction; they see "VetCert pending" and don't realize you're certified.
- **Recertification lapse (3-year rule)**: SDVOSB certification expires ~3 years from approval. Calendar alert at 2029-02-01 (90 days before expected expiration) is non-negotiable.

### 5.1 The Sole-Source Opportunity

The SDVOSB sole-source threshold is $4M for services and $7M for manufacturing. A contracting officer can award sole-source when they reasonably expect fewer than two SDVOSBs can perform the work. For specialized cryptographic evidence hardware with active patent portfolio, that bar is easy to meet.

In FY2025, approximately 35% of all SDVOSB dollars ($10B of $28.6B) were awarded sole-source. **The strategy is not to wait for competitive solicitations — it is to position ZKNOT with agency contracting officers before a solicitation is posted so they reach for a sole-source vehicle instead.**

Target sole-source sequence:
1. **VA Vets First** for medical device attestation / supply chain integrity (requires VIP registration first)
2. **DHS cybersecurity evidence hardware** (USB power path security has direct DHS application)
3. **DoD unit-level chain-of-custody tools** (USSOCOM via SOFWERX; AFWERX for UAV applications)

### 5.2 SDVOSB Status in Acquirer Conversations

The SDVOSB certification belongs in the **first line** of the one-page executive summary: *"ZKNOT, INC. is a certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) building cryptographic evidence hardware."*

Frame the SDVOSB advantage per acquirer type:

- **Defense / intelligence acquirers (L3Harris, CACI, Leidos, Palantir)**: SDVOSB acquisition gives them a set-aside vehicle inside their supply chain. The target can pursue contracts their prime divisions cannot pursue directly.
- **Forensics acquirers (Axon, MSAB, Cellebrite)**: VA Vets First and federal law enforcement contracts are accessible channels. SDVOSB removes competition from most federal bids.
- **Enterprise acquirers (Veritas)**: SDVOSB provides a federal market entry point they may not have. Business development asset, not just compliance status.
